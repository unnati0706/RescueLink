How It Works

When an unexpected vehicular failure occurs, manual positioning updates are prone to mistakes. Our unified framework uses exact coordinates to optimize dispatch routes and keep communication clear.

1

Precision Geo-Sourcing

Instantly pulls live device parameters to pin down exact locations along open highways, avoiding confusing text-only address structures.

2

Transparent Escrow Pricing

Itemizes taxes, distance fees, and flat rates directly within localized currency structures (₹) before a driver is dispatched to prevent sudden price surges.

3

Fail-Safe SOS Architecture

Keeps emergency vehicle profiles active and notifies pre-configured emergency contacts simultaneously if high-risk collisions occur.

4

Automated Audit Trails

Saves clear history logs, transaction summaries, and digital receipts to ensure complete transparency for personal or insurance records.
